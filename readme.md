<h3>Round 1 Submission</h3>

CivicEye is a modern, visual web platform designed to bridge the gap between citizens and municipal authorities. It transforms the traditional, boring process of filing complaints into an engaging, community-driven "newspaper" experience.

<p style="font-weight: bold;">üö© 1. The Issue</p>
Why did we build this?

Opacity in Governance: Citizens often file complaints on government portals but never know the status of their request.

Outdated UI: Existing grievance portals are often text-heavy, clunky, and difficult to navigate on mobile devices.

Lack of Community: Reporting an issue feels like an indivisual task. Neighbors don't know if someone else has already reported the pothole on their street, leading to duplicate reports or no report at all.

<p style="font-weight: bold;">üí° 2. The Solution </p>
"Instagram meets The Daily News" Simply means that the web portal is inspired from popular social media platforms and provides a similar engaging experience!

CivicEye reimagines civic engagement by combining the scrollable, visual appeal of social media with the authority and detail of a newspaper layout.

Visual-First Reporting: Every complaint focuses on photographic and videographic evidence.

Transparent Status Tracking: Clear badges (Pending, In Progress, Resolved) let users see impact immediately.

Community Validation: An "Upvote" system allows the community to highlight the most urgent issues, transforming the "Like" button to a functional "Support" metric.

Newspaper Layout: A clean, serif-font design that lends credibility and seriousness to the issues reported.

<p style="font-weight: bold;">üöÄ 4. Features Delivered</p>
Interactive Home Feed: A scrollable list of complaints with a "Newspaper" aesthetic.

Smart Sidebar: Navigation with active state highlighting and a sticky account menu.

Publishing System: A drag-and-drop image upload interface with live preview and detailed form validation.

My Reports: A filtered history view showing only the logged-in user's submissions with status indicators.
